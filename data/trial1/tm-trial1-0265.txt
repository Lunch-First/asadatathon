Cable news networks' ratings skyrocketed Saturday between 10 and 11 p.m. for the verdict in the George Zimmerman (l.) murder trial. Fox News Channel topped its rivals with an average of 3.7 million viewers, followed by CNN's 3.407 million. HLN placed third with 2.2 million viwers and MSNBC brought up the rear with 1.3 million. A jury found Zimmerman not guilty in the shooting death of teen Trayvon Martin.
