THOUSANDS of protesters called for justice for the family of shot black teen Trayvon Martin - as stars yesterday blasted the acquittal of his vigilante killer.
Dozens were arrested in demos across America, including Times Square in New York, above. In LA, crowds hurled missiles at cops and officers fired rubber bullets.
There were protests in Florida where neighbourhood watch volunteer George Zimmerman, 29, was cleared of murdering Trayvon. On Twitter, furious hip-hop star Chris Brown, 24, branded the verdict "Bulls***!" Superstar Rihanna, 25, wrote: "This is the saddest news ever!!! Only in America." Beyonce Knowles' sister Solange held a rally for 17-year-old Trayvon in New York.
