UNITED STATES: Police say the FBI is taking over the investigation of a suburban police officer who fatally shot an unarmed teenager.
Police Chief Tom Jackson said he was informed that the FBI was going to carry out the investigation into the death of 18-year-old Michael Brown, near St Louis, Missouri.
Police say the teenager was shot multiple times on Saturday in a scuffle with an officer.
Tensions erupted in Ferguson after a candlelight vigil on Sunday night turned violent.
