UNITED STATES: Protests in a US suburb rocked by racial unrest after a white police officer killed unarmed black teenager Michael Brown  turned violent. Molotov cocktails were hurled at police, who responded with smoke bombs and tear gas to disperse the crowd in Ferguson, Missouri.
Police chief Thomas Jackson said  his department welcomed Justice Department training on race relations in the St Louis suburb, where two thirds of the 21,000 residents are black, while all but three of the police force's 53 officers are white.
