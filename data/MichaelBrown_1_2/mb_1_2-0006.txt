A DEMONSTRATOR throws back a tear gas container after police tried to break up a group of bystanders during riots in Missouri, US.
Heavily-armed cops have been clashing with protesters since the police killing of black teenager Michael Brown in Ferguson, a suburb of St Louis, on Saturday. President Barack Obama yesterday urged police not to use "excessive force" after widespread criticism of a military-style crackdown.
Missouri governor Jay Nixon said there would be an "operational shift" to the city's law enforcement.
