MISSOURI: Nights of unrest have vied with calls for calm in a St Louis suburb where an unarmed black teenager was killed by police.
And the community is still pressing for answers about the weekend shooting.
Hundreds of residents packed two churches for community meetings about the death of Michael Brown (18), who was confronted by a Ferguson officer while walking with a friend in the street near his grandmother''s home. St Louis County Police Department is investigating.
