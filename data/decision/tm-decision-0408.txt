DAYTONA BEACH -- Local leaders will participate in a Trayvon Martin Community Forum on Thursday organized by the Daytona Beach Black Clergy Alliance and NAACP at the Greater Friendship Baptist Church.
Included in the panel are Daytona Beach Mayor Derrick Henry, Daytona Beach Police Chief Mike Chitwood, local NAACP president Cynthia Slater, NAACP member Thaddeus Collins, president of the Daytona Beach Black Clergy Alliance Rev. L. Ronald Durham and Daytona Times writer James Harper.
The panel will be moderated by Claudette McFadden, associate provost in the office of the president of Bethune-Cookman University.
Greater Friendship Baptist Church is at 539 George W. Engram Blvd. The forum will start at 6:30 p.m.
