POLICE shot three young men who had just left a bachelor party at a New York strip club early yesterday, killing one man on the day of his wedding, according to police and witnesses.
One of the survivors was in critical condition. The shooting happened just before dawn near Club Kalua in the Queens borough, said Officer Kathleen Price. She said there were no reports that any officers were wounded.
