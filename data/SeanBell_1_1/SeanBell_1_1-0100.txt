Eileen MCKELLAR, May (nee Westwood) - Eileen passed away after a strong, courageous battle with breast cancer and then, alzheimers, she was 86 years old. Eileen was born on April 3, 1920 in Galt, Cambridge and for the last two years, was a resident of Forest Heights Long Term Care Centre, Kitchener. Beloved mother of Edward "Ted" Coverett and his wife, Marilyn Coverett, known to Eileen only as her daughter, never as daughter-in-law.  
She will be deeply and sadly missed by her daughter, Linda Coleman and her pride and joy; her special grandchildren, Susan Coleman, Jodie Coleman, Kim Coleman, 
 her specially loved grandson, Jeff Coverett and her great-grandson, Zebadiah and Keagan Coverett. Eileen had a total of eight grandchildren and nine great-grandchildren and one on its way. She is survived by one sister, Marg Westwood and family and friends. Predeceased by her husband, Roy McKellar (1993); also by brothers, Alf Harry and Frank Westwood. So farewell to a very special Mom and Grandmom, our Munchkin Mom. "Flying high over your rainbow now, with your heavenly Father!" Friends and family may call at the Ratz-Bechtel Funeral Home, 621 King St. W., Kitchener, on Thursday from 7-9 p.m. The complete funeral service will be held in Ratz-Bechtel Chapel on Friday, December 1, 2006 at 1 p.m. Cremation to follow.
