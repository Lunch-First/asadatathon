Rumsfeld exposed
MADRID: Outgoing US defence secretary Donald Rumsfeld authorised the mistreatment of detainees at Abu Ghraib in Iraq, the prison's former commander Janis Karpinski has revealed. The former US army brigadier general told El Pais newspaper she had seen a memorandum signed by Rumsfeld approving harsh interrogation methods.

Brainy whales
WASHINGTON: Humpback whales could be more intelligent than they are given credit for after US researchers found they had a type of brain cell seen only in humans, the great apes, and other cetaceans such as dolphins. The finding may help to explain some whale behaviour, such as their intricate communication skills.
