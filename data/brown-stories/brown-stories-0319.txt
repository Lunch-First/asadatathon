 Office of the Senator Claire McCaskill, U.S Government has issued the following news release:
Following the shooting of teenager 
 by a Ferguson, Mo. police officer, U.S. Senator Claire McCaskill today spoke directly with Molly Moran, Acting Assistant Attorney General for Civil Rights at the Department of Justice on the status of a federal civil rights investigation, and released the following statement: 
"As we continue to grieve for Michael and his family, I'm working to make sure the federal piece of this investigation is fair, transparent, and moving forward without delay. As it proceeds, I'll be remaining in close touch with the Department of Justice, and am reaching out personally to local elected officials and religious leaders, to keep monitoring the painful events on the ground in Ferguson."
For further information please visit: http://www.mccaskill.senate.gov
